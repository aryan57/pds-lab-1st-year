Compute 1!+2!+3!+...+i! for i=1 to n without recursive function
User gives a positive integer, n, as input.
Assume that n is at most 12.
Compute 1!+2!+3!+...+i! for i=1 to n without any recursive function and print them on the terminal.

Examples:
Enter n: 6
1 3 9 33 153 873
Enter n: 10
1 3 9 33 153 873 5913 46233 409113 4037913
